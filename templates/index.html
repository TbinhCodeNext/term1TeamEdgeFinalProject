Welcome to Raspieats!

Here are the rules to the game:

  1. Using the SenseHat's Joystick (Trinket: arrow keys) to move.
  2. Aim to get/eat the cyan dot.

    3. If you choose this path, you can move anywhere on the board.

    You are to get the dot for 10 times. (Please don't get bored.)

Welcome to Catchem!

Here are the rules to the game:

  1. Using the SenseHat's Joystick (Trinket: arrow keys) to move.
  2. Aim to catch the dots before it reach the bottom.

    3. If you choose this path, you can only move left or right on the board.
